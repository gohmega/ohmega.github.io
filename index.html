what is it

ohmega stands for { Open-source Halcyonic Method of Educating a Global Academy }

learn

learning from someone of your generation is more effective and fun than the usual method of teaching. you understand better and work efficiently. rectifying one's mistake without hurting their feelings is important.

be happy

happiness is not materialistic. it is achieved by helping one another. in this lms, you can help your peers, and also yourself by learning subjects which you didn't understand. the site is always filled with life. it's like a social network. get your doubts clarified with ease.

make it fun

studying should be done with interest, otherwise it becomes sulky... try to simplify stuff and make studying fun. do not study for the sake of studying. playing is also important, don't sacrifice it at any cost, but do limit it. try studying as if you are doing a project or an activity, it's better that way.

portfolio of ohmega inc.

ohmega inc. is a learning management system which is completely open source. it is a place where students teach one another. it is made for students by a student. our motto is "learn | ∞".

ohmega inc. was built with an intention to make studying fun. alongside which, students will also incubate leadership skills and build their social profile. it trys to make people down to earth and become good citizens of their country. apart from that, ohmega helps young learners to understand what they are studying in school rather than studying for the sake of it. making people happy, is another factor that is involved in the building of ohmega inc.

rules and guidelines

rules are not made to be broken, but can be altered in a sensible way.
here are some guidelines to be followed.

guidelines of ohmega inc.

  • nobody is a role model; try to inspire yourself.
  • nobody is gonna screw you unless you do something horribly wrong.
  • simplify stuff to make it easier to understand.
  • do not take refuge in mugging things up, it only makes it worse.
  • try to be down to earth, it makes people happy.
  • help others with no form of discrimination.
  • don't think that money can always buy 'happiness', be simple.
  • in ohmega, there is no such thing as a 'status' level. all are equal under the sun.
  • treat everyone with respect. do not expect anything from anyone. it only brings about anger.
  • honesty is the best policy. do not try to be others, be yourself at all times.
  • do not copy. plagiarism is a big no. be authentic in whatever you do.
  • don't cheat others; by doing so, you cheat yourself.
  • be trustworthy. don't break anyone's trust.
  • be happy, and make others happy
  • swearing is not allowed. why get angry?
